Projects I've worked on

While working for OpenForum Europe (OFE), I completely redesigned and developed the new OFE site using a custom WordPress theme. I also organised the migration of content from the old site running on Plone.

COIS

The Community for Open Interoperability Standards (COIS) is a UK-based industry organisation working to promote the use of Open Standards in all levels of government. I designed and built a simple landing page for them using Bootstrap.

Indius is the most popular French speaking news site devoted exclusively to indie video games. I developed their top interractive banner and helped to fix various display bugs on their WordPress site.

As part of a campaign to publicise OFE's recommendations for the European Digital Single Market Strategy, I created a simple site including some basic infographics, using an existing WordPress theme.

My skills

I have good design skills (Photoshop, Illustrator, ...) and experience creating wireframes and visual mock-ups for websites.
